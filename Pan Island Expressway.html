    • The Pan Island Expressway

    The Pan Island Expressway (PIE), when completed in 2017, is a 25km open-toll expressway linking the eastern coast of Pulau Tiong Ring Road (PTRR) and provides connectivity to Myna Town and suburbs along the eastern coast.
